About Me

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."

With over 8 years of experience in digital design and development, I've cultivated a unique approach that merges aesthetic sensibility with technical expertise. My work spans across multiple disciplines including UX/UI design, brand identity, and frontend development.

Based in New York City, I collaborate with clients globally to create impactful digital solutions that drive results. My design philosophy centers on creating meaningful experiences that resonate with users while achieving business objectives.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring photography, practicing yoga, or hunting for vintage typography books in hidden bookstores.

My Goals

Master Advanced Animation Techniques

Currently focused on mastering advanced CSS and JavaScript animation techniques to create more engaging and interactive user experiences. Exploring libraries like GSAP and Three.js to implement 3D elements into web projects.

Expand Design System Expertise

Working to deepen my knowledge of creating comprehensive design systems that scale across multiple platforms. Focusing on component libraries, documentation standards, and accessibility implementation.

Launch Design Education Platform

Planning to create an online platform to share design knowledge and mentor emerging designers. Currently developing curriculum and recording initial content for courses on UX fundamentals and brand identity design.

Explore Sustainable Design Practices

Beginning to research and implement sustainable design practices in my workflow. Investigating how digital design choices impact energy consumption and exploring ways to create more environmentally conscious digital products.
